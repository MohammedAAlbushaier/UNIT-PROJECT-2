Introduction

Quran Care Community ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

Your Rights

  • The right to object to or restrict processing
